Welcome to GitHub Pages.

I received my BS degree in Computer Science with honors from Chu Kechen colleage of Zhenjiang University in 2004 and moved onto Hong Kong University of Science and Technology for my PhD degree. During my PhD study, I worked in VisGraph Labs under the advisement of Dr. Philip Fu and Dr. Pedro Sander. I completed my thesis defense in 2010 and then went to National Unveristy of Singapore to fill a Postdoc position. I came to AMD Shanghai in 2011. Now I am a MTS researcher in advanced GPU technology research team. My research topics ranges from real-time rendering and GPGPU architecture and applications.
